HIFU (High Intensity Focused Ultrasound)

HIFU is a non-surgical skin rejuvenation treatment that uses ultrasound technology to lift, firm, and tighten skin by stimulating collagen production deep beneath the surface. Known as a 'non-surgical facelift', it provides a rejuvenated and more youthful appearance.

Key Benefits

  • Proven safety record for all skin colors
  • Non-invasive alternative to surgical procedures
  • Stimulates natural collagen production
  • Lifts and tightens sagging skin
Treatment Duration
30-90 minutes per session
Downtime
Virtually none

Ideal For

Sagging or loose skin on face, neck, and body; Fine lines and wrinkles; Signs of aging; Skin laxity
